What We Are?

Shale Pizza was first opened by two friends Aamna and Sadia with accompany of their friends Ayesha and Mahnoor in The City Of Lights, Karachi, Pakistan in 2010. These four girls created an innovative, high quality, engaging, informal eating experience in a friendly neighborhood restaurant.Shale Pizza became a popular place with the teenage and college crowd. Shale Pizza attracted families that enjoyed a night out eating a meal that they would not be having at home. After the families left, Shale Pizza transformed itself into a hangout for college-age kids. Having a good time was an essential part of Shale Pizza. The idea caught on and the second branch of Shale Pizza was opened in 2012 and by 2017, there are three branches of Shale Pizza at the main locations of the city.

At Shale Pizza we are committed to provide you with fast, friendly service, great value of food, beverages. Once your pizza is ordered, its action a plenty. Using our fresh healthy dough recipe, the stretcher starts stretching the base, great viewing in itself. The makers add the right amount of ingredients, which have all been freshly prepared on our premises, into the woodfire oven and then the magic happens. Fresh, healthy and woodfired.

Shale Pizza during the core of its existence has gradually aided in the development of various local suppliers who as a requirement have to supply food staff equivalent in quality to the international food standards. In this regard, our restaurant chain has proved to be a major contributor towards the progress of the Food Industry of our city as a whole by bringing it to a point where they can now look forward for supplying their items to other.
